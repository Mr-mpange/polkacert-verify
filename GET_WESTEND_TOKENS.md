## Troubleshooting

### Tokens not showing up?
- Wait 1-2 minutes
- Refresh the page
- Check you used the correct address
- Try the faucet again

### Faucet says "Rate limited"?
- You already got tokens recently
- Wait 24 hours
- Try alternative faucets
- Ask in Discord

### Wrong network?
- Make sure you're on Westend (not Polkadot mainnet)
- Check your .env file: `VITE_POLKADOT_ENDPOINT=wss://westend-rpc.polkadot.io`
